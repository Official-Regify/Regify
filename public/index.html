Welcome to Regify

Managing Public Events Shouldn’t Be Chaos.

From college fests to public gatherings, Regify is the easiest way to organize, assign, and execute tasks with your team.



What is Regify?

Regify is a web-based event manager built for teams that organize large events like college fests, public shows, or gatherings. It helps you:

"
  • Create events with roles and responsibilities
  • Assign tasks to team members
  • Track progress in real-time
  • Avoid miscommunication and last-minute mess-ups

Why Regify?

  • Role-based Team Management Assign different roles — volunteers, coordinators, heads — with permission control.

  • Task Tracking & Deadlines Track who's doing what and when it’s due — no more lost WhatsApp messages.

  • File & Resource Sharing Upload, share, and manage resources in one place.

  • Smart Updates Auto-reminders and activity logs to keep your team in sync.
